## Saudi Exchange

The Saudi Exchange (Tadawul) is the sole entity authorised to act as the securities exchange in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. It lists Main Market and Nomu parallel market equities, sukuk and bonds, exchange traded funds, and real estate investment trusts, and runs trading on a Nasdaq X-stream matching engine.
