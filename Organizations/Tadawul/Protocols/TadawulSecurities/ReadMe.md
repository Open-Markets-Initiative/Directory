## Tadawul Securities


Saudi Exchange cash securities market covering Main Market and Nomu parallel market equities, sukuk and bonds, exchange traded funds, and real estate investment trusts traded on the Nasdaq X-stream platform.


### Protocols


| Protocol | Type | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Itch](Itch.md) | MarketData | Saudi Exchange X-stream Itch Order By Order Market Data |
