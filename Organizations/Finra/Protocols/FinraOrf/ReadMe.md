## Finra ORF


Finra Over The Counter Reporting Facility for reporting trades in Nasdaq and other OTC equity securities executed outside of an exchange.


### Protocols


| Protocol | Type | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Atds](Atds.md) | MarketData | Finra Trace Agency Debt Trade Dissemination |
| [Tdds](Tdds.md) | MarketData | Finra Orf Otc Equity Trade Dissemination |
