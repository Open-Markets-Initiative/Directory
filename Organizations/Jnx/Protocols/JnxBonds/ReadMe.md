## Japannext Bonds


Japannext Proprietary Trading System bonds market offering electronic trading of Japanese government and corporate bonds outside of the primary exchange.


### Protocols


| Protocol | Type | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Itch](Itch.md) | MarketData | Japannext Pts Bonds Order By Order Market Data |
| [Ouch](Ouch.md) | OrderEntry | Japannext Pts Bonds Order Entry |
