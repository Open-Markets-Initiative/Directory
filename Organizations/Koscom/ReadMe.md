## Koscom Co., Ltd.

Korea Securities Computing Corporation (Koscom). Established in 1977 by the Korea Ministry of Finance and the Korea Exchange (KRX), which remains its largest shareholder. Koscom operates the technology infrastructure for Korean capital markets and distributes KRX market data to financial institutions through its Market Data Service (MDCS) at data.koscom.co.kr.
