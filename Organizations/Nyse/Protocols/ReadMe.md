## Nyse Protocols

Protocol specifications for New York Stock Exchange (Nyse) trading platform including Nyse, Nyse Arca, Nyse American, Nyse National, and Nyse Chicago.

| Protocol | Type | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [Pillar](Pillar.md) | Trading Platform | BinaryGateway for equities and options order entry and market data |
