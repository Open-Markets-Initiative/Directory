## AsxSecurities T24: Asx 24 Itch Market Data

Binary market data protocol distributing real-time order, trade, and reference data for interest rate derivatives and futures traded on the Asx 24 platform.

### Overview

T24 Itch is the binary market data protocol for the Asx 24 trading platform, providing real-time order book and trade data for interest rate futures and options. The feed delivers order-by-order add, modify, and delete events, trade messages, and reference data for all instruments listed on the Asx 24 platform, enabling subscribers to reconstruct the complete order book.

The service is built around three components. A multicast feed disseminates the real-time Itch-style event stream. A Blink Tcp recovery service replays messages missed on multicast. A Glance Tcp snapshot service publishes the current book state on request so that subscribers can resynchronize mid-day or bootstrap without having to process the full day of events.

### Transport

Udp multicast for the live Asx 24 Itch feed, carrying heartbeat-paced sequenced binary messages for real-time delivery of order and trade events. Tcp to the Blink recovery service for replay of missed multicast messages and to the Glance snapshot service for on-demand full book snapshots.

### Key Characteristics

- **Interest rate derivatives** - Futures and options on Asx interest rate products
- **Order-by-order** - Full depth of book reconstruction from individual order events
- **Multicast delivery** - Real-time Ip multicast distribution of the market data stream
- **Blink recovery** - Tcp replay service for gap recovery of missed multicast messages
- **Glance snapshot** - Tcp snapshot service for on-demand full book initialisation
- **Heartbeat paced** - Periodic heartbeat messages on the multicast feed
- **Binary encoded** - Compact fixed-width messages for low latency processing
