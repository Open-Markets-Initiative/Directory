## T24 Itch: Asx Securities Market Data

T24 Itch market data feed for Asx interest rate derivatives on the T24 platform.

### Overview

T24 Itch is the binary market data protocol for the Asx T24 trading platform, providing real-time order book and trade data for interest rate derivatives. The feed delivers order-by-order messages allowing subscribers to reconstruct the complete order book for futures and options on interest rate products.

The protocol provides order add, modify, and delete events, trade messages, and reference data for all instruments listed on the T24 platform. Subscribers use the sequential message stream to build and maintain order book state for pricing, trading, and risk management applications.

### Transport

Udp multicast with sequence numbers for gap detection and snapshot feeds for recovery.

### Key Characteristics

- **Interest rate derivatives** - Futures and options on Asx interest rate products
- **Order-by-order** - Individual order events for complete book reconstruction
- **Full depth of book** - Every order visible at every price level
- **Binary encoded** - Compact fixed-length messages for efficient processing
