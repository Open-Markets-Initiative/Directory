## Opra: Options Price Reporting Authority

Consolidated market data protocol disseminating options quote and trade information from all Us options exchanges.

### Overview

The Options Price Reporting Authority (Opra) is the Siac-operated consolidated options market data feed collecting and disseminating real-time quote and trade information from all participating Us options exchanges. Opra provides the authoritative source of consolidated options market data including best bid and offer quotes, last sale trade reports, and open interest for all listed options series.

Opra operates with two participant roles. Obi recipients receive the consolidated output feed containing merged options market data from all exchanges. Odi participants are the options exchanges that submit their quotation and trade data as input to the Opra system for consolidation and redistribution. The protocol handles the high message rates generated by the Us options market across millions of active series.

### Transport

Multicast Udp for Obi output distribution to recipients. Dedicated connections for Odi input submission from participating options exchanges. Sequenced delivery with gap detection.

### Key Characteristics

- **Consolidated options data** - Quotes and trades from all Us options exchanges
- **Obi recipients** - Subscribers receiving the consolidated output feed
- **Odi participants** - Options exchanges submitting input data
- **All listed options** - Covers all series across all Us options exchanges
- **High message volume** - Designed for the high throughput of Us options markets
- **Regulatory feed** - Consolidated options data mandated by the Options Plan
