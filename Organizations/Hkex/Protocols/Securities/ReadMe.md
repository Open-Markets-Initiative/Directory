## Hkex Securities Market


The Hkex Securities Market is operated by The Stock Exchange of Hong Kong Limited (SEHK), a wholly owned subsidiary of Hong Kong Exchanges and Clearing (Hkex). It covers all cash-market listed securities including ordinary shares, preference shares, rights, depository receipts, SPAC shares, derivative warrants, CBBCs, inline warrants, equity warrants, ETFs, REITs, unit trusts, leveraged and inverse products, and debt securities. Market data is disseminated via the Orion Market Data - Cash (OMD-C) platform.
