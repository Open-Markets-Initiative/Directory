## Hkex China Connect


Hkex's Northbound Stock Connect programme, providing Hong Kong-based investors with access to eligible A-Share securities traded on the Shanghai Stock Exchange (SSE) and Shenzhen Stock Exchange (SZSE). Market data for eligible securities is disseminated via the OMD-CC (Orion Market Data - China Connect) platform.
