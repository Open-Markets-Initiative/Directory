## Osaka Digital Exchange

Osaka Digital Exchange Co., Ltd. (ODX) is a Japanese securities exchange that operates proprietary trading systems (PTS) for equities and security tokens. Founded by SBI Holdings, ODX operates an equities PTS and the START platform for digital securities trading.
