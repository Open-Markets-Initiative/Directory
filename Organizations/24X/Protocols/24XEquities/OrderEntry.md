## 24XEquities Order Entry: 24X Fix Order Entry (MEMO FIX)

Financial Information eXchange (Fix) encoding of the MEMO order entry protocol for the 24X National Securities Exchange, used by members to submit, modify and cancel orders and receive execution reports over the Memx-Tcp Fix session.

### Transport

Tcp via the Memx-Tcp Fix session for reliable ordered delivery of order entry, modification, cancellation, and execution report messages.
