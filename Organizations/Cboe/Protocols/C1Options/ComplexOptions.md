## Cboe Options Complex Options

Pitch feed providing market data for complex options instruments including strategy definitions and order activity on Cboe Options Exchange.

### Overview

Options Complex Options delivers real-time market data for complex options instruments traded on Cboe Options Exchange. The feed provides strategy creation notifications, order activity, and execution data for user-defined and exchange-defined multi-leg combinations.

The feed includes instrument creation messages as new complex strategies are defined, along with ongoing market data for active complex instruments.

### Transport

Udp multicast with sequenced delivery and spin server gap recovery.

### Key Characteristics

- **Strategy creation** - Notifications when new complex instruments are defined
- **Order activity** - Real-time order and execution data for complex instruments
- **Multi-leg support** - Coverage of user-defined and exchange-defined strategy combinations
