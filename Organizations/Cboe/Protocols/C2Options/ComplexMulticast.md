## Cboe C2 Options Complex Multicast

Pitch multicast feed providing combined market data for complex multi-leg options strategies on Cboe C2 Options Exchange.

### Overview

Options Complex Multicast delivers a combined stream of complex options strategy market data including book updates, quotes, and trades over Udp multicast. The feed consolidates complex strategy information into a single multicast delivery channel for simplified consumption.

The feed serves participants who want comprehensive complex strategy data from a unified source rather than subscribing to separate complex depth, top, and auction feeds individually.

### Transport

Udp multicast with sequenced delivery and spin server gap recovery.

### Key Characteristics

- **Combined complex data** - Unified stream of complex strategy market data
- **Multicast delivery** - Efficient Udp multicast distribution for complex instruments
- **Strategy coverage** - Multi-leg options strategies on Cboe C2 Options Exchange
- **Simplified consumption** - Single feed for complex book, quote, and trade data
