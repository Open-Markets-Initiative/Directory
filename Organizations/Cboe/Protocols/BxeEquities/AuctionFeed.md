## BxeEquities Auction Feed: Cboe Europe BXE Auction Feed (Ascii Pitch over Soup 2.0)

Cboe Auction Feed (CAF) — Ascii Pitch over Soup 2.0 disseminating Opening, Closing and Periodic auction indicative price/size and auction summary results. Wire messages are common to the Cboe Europe BXE and CXE books.

### Transport

Tcp delivery via Soup 2.0 framing — fixed-length non-control Ascii records; the Soup envelope handles login, heartbeat, sequencing and replay.
