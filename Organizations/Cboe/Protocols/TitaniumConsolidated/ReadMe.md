## Cboe Titanium Consolidated


Cboe Titanium Consolidated market data — cross-exchange aggregated feeds (Cboe One Equities and Cboe One Options) that combine trade and quote activity across the four Cboe US equities exchanges (BYX, BZX, EDGA, EDGX), the Cboe Canada trading books (MATCHNow, NEO-L, NEO-N, NEO-D), and the four Cboe US options exchanges (BZX Options, C1 Options, C2 Options, EDGX Options).


### Protocols


| Protocol | Type | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [OneEquities](OneEquities.md) | MarketData | Cboe One Equities |
| [OneOptions](OneOptions.md) | MarketData | Cboe One Options |
