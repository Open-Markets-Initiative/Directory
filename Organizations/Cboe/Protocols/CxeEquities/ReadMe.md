## Cboe CXE


Cboe Europe CXE lit order book (MIC CHIX), a pan-European regulated equities and ETF market operated by Cboe Europe out of the Equinix Slough (LD4) and LD3 data centres.


### Protocols


| Protocol | Type | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [AuctionFeed](AuctionFeed.md) | MarketData | Cboe Europe CXE Auction Feed (Ascii Pitch over Soup 2.0) |
| [LastSale](LastSale.md) | MarketData | Cboe Europe CXE Last Sale Trade Tape (Ascii Pitch over Soup 2.0) |
| [MulticastDepthOfBook](MulticastDepthOfBook.md) | MarketData | Cboe Europe CXE Full Depth Of Book Data |
| [TcpDepthOfBook](TcpDepthOfBook.md) | MarketData | Cboe Europe CXE Tcp Depth Of Book (Ascii Pitch over Soup 2.0) |
