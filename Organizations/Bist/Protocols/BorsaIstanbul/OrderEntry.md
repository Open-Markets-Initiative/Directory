## BorsaIstanbul Order Entry: Borsa Istanbul Genium INET FIX order entry

Financial Information eXchange (Fix) order entry interface of the Nasdaq Genium INET trading platform for Borsa Istanbul, carrying the full order lifecycle, quoting, trade capture reporting, and allocation messages across equity, derivatives, and fixed income markets.

### Transport

Tcp for authenticated Fix sessions with sequence-number gap recovery via Resend Request and Sequence Reset.
