## Borsa İstanbul A.Ş.

Borsa İstanbul (BIST) is the sole securities exchange entity of Türkiye, formed in 2013 through the merger of the Istanbul Stock Exchange, the Istanbul Gold Exchange, and the Derivatives Exchange of Turkey (VOB). It operates equity, debt securities, derivatives (VIOP), and precious metals markets on its BISTECH trading platform, jointly developed with Nasdaq and derived from Nasdaq's Genium INET technology.
