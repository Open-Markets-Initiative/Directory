## OTC Link NQB


OTC Link NQB is OTC Markets Group's inter-dealer quotation system (IDQS) for daytime OTC dealer-quoted equities. Successor branding to the historical National Quotation Bureau (NQB). Order Acceptance at 6 AM, Pre-Market Open at 8 AM, Market Open at 9:30 AM, After Hours at 4 PM, Market Close at 5 PM. Publishes the order-based OTC Link NQB Multicast Data Feeds using the shared Otc Link encoding; Top of Book data is also republished into the OTC Link ATS feed.


### Protocols


| Protocol | Type | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| [DepthOfBook](DepthOfBook.md) | MarketData | Order-by-order full depth channel for OTC Link NQB |
| [Retransmission](Retransmission.md) | MarketData | TCP binary retransmission service for OTC Link NQB recovery |
| [TopOfBook](TopOfBook.md) | MarketData | Aggregated top-of-book channel for OTC Link NQB |
