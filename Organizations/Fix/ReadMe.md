## FIX Trading Community

The FIX Trading Community is a non-profit, industry-driven standards body at the heart of global trading. The organization maintains the Financial Information eXchange (FIX) Protocol, the messaging standard used by the majority of the world's exchanges, banks, broker-dealers, and investment managers for trade communication.
